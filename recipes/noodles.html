Veg Noodles

Description

Veg noodles are a delightful and vibrant dish that brings together the goodness of fresh vegetables and the bold flavors of Indo-Chinese cuisine. This recipe involves stir-frying boiled noodles with a medley of colorful, crunchy vegetables like carrots, capsicum, cabbage, and beans, which not only add nutritional value but also enhance the visual appeal of the dish. The flavors are elevated with a combination of savory soy sauce, tangy vinegar, and a hint of chili sauce for spice.

Quick to make and incredibly versatile, veg noodles are a perfect choice for a satisfying meal, whether it's a quick lunch, dinner, or even a party snack. They are loved by people of all ages and can be customized easily by adding your favorite vegetables or tofu. This dish combines the textures of soft noodles and crisp vegetables, all tossed in a wok over high heat, giving it an authentic smoky flavor. Whether served as a standalone dish or alongside other Indo-Chinese favorites, veg noodles are a crowd-pleaser and a go-to recipe for those looking for something delicious and wholesome.

Ingredients

  • Noodles:

    200 grams (any type, preferably thin or Hakka noodles)

  • Vegetables:

    • Cabbage (shredded): 1 cup
    • Carrot (julienned): 1 medium
    • Capsicum (sliced): 1 medium
    • Green beans (thinly sliced): 6-8
    • Spring onions (chopped): 2-3 stalks

  • Garlic:

    4-5 cloves (finely chopped)

  • Ginger:

    1-inch piece (grated or finely chopped)

  • Soy Sauce:

    2 tbsp

  • Vinegar:

    1 tbsp

  • Chili Sauce:

    1 tbsp (adjust to taste)

  • Black Pepper Powder:

    1/2 tsp

  • Salt:

    To taste

  • Oil:

    2 tbsp (preferably sesame or vegetable oil)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the Noodles:
    • Bring water to a boil in a large pot. Add a pinch of salt and a few drops of oil.
    • Add the noodles and cook as per package instructions (usually 4-5 minutes).
    • Drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to prevent sticking. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Wash and cut all the vegetables as described above. Keep them ready for stir-frying.
  3. Heat the Wok/Pan:
    • Heat oil in a large wok or pan over high heat.
    • Add chopped garlic and ginger. Saut√© for 30 seconds until aromatic.
  4. Stir-Fry the Vegetables:
    • Add the vegetables (cabbage, carrots, beans, capsicum) and stir-fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes. They should remain crunchy.
  5. Add Sauces and Seasoning:
    • Lower the heat slightly and add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, black pepper, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Combine Noodles and Vegetables:
    • Add the boiled noodles to the wok. Toss everything together gently to coat the noodles with the sauces and vegetables evenly.
  7. Finish and Garnish:
    • Sprinkle chopped spring onions and mix lightly.
  8. Serve Hot:
    • Serve immediately as a main dish or a side with your favorite Indo-Chinese dish.
